I am a postdoctoral research associate at Princeton University, New Jersey, United States. I obtained my PhD from the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Australian National University, Canberra, Australia in 2022. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore in June 2018, where I majored in Physics.

I am mainly interested in astrophysical fluid dynamics and numerical simulations of astrophysical systems. I currently work on simulations of turbulence in galaxy and galaxy cluster halos.

I have used AthenaK, PLUTO, and FLASH for my numerical simulations.
